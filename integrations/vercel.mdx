### Setup Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Vercel Account">
    Visit [Vercel Dashboard](https://vercel.com/dashboard) and ensure you're logged in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create API Token">
    Go to **Settings > Tokens** and click **"Create Token"**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure in CodinIT">
    Navigate to **Settings > Deployments** and add your Vercel token.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Repository (Optional)">
    Import your GitHub/GitLab repository for automatic deployments.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy Application">
    Click **"Deploy to Vercel"** to launch your application on the edge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Configuration Options

<ParamField name="VERCEL_ACCESS_TOKEN" type="string" required>
  Your Vercel access token. Get this from [Vercel Tokens](https://vercel.com/account/tokens).
</ParamField>

<ParamField name="VERCEL_TEAM_ID" type="string">
  Team ID for organization deployments. Leave empty for personal projects.
</ParamField>
